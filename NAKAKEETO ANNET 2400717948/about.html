About Our Cooperative

Learn how we are transforming lives through community empowerment and financial inclusion

Project Description

Our Community-Based cooperative Project is designed to empower low-income community members.
By encouraging collective savings and providing access to affordable credit.

The cooperative offers saving services,small loans,and financial literacy training, enabling participants.
to invest income-generating activities,pay school fees,and improve household welfare

Our Motivation

This project is motivated by the urgent need to address poverty and financial exclusion in our community.
Many households struggle to access reliable sources of income and face barriers to traditional financial services.

By establishing this cooperative,we empower members to pool their resources,access low-interest loans, and create sustainable livelihoods that break the cycle of poverty.

Who We Serve
